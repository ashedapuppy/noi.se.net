## alphabet soup

1) make soup base\
    I like to use homemade chicken bouillon, but if you don't have any you can use store bought, I promise I won't tell anyone
2) add veggies and protein\
    add some veggies into the soup to make it nice and filling, cabbage is nice for some added msg, but anything goes
3) SEASON IT\
    no, salt and peper is not enough.
    add some smoked paprika maybe, sprinkle on a drop chili oil if you're feeling funny
    remember that most spices taste 1000% better if you dry cook them for a few minutes first,
    also oil is great cos it floats on the liquid and adds an extra layer of flavour and texture,
    sesame oil or chili oil (laoganma is the best) are your best friends here.
4) add you noodles\
    remember to take out all the z from the alphavet pasta because z makes you eepy, and you don't wanna fall asleep while eating the pasta.
5) share with friends\
6) enjoy!\
